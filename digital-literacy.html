Digital Literacy for Seniors

Bridging the technology gap across generations

About This Program

Since 2019, we've helped over 1,200 seniors gain confidence with technology. Our volunteers provide patient, one-on-one assistance to help older adults stay connected with loved ones and access essential services online.

Volunteer Opportunities

  • Tech Tutoring: Weekly 1-hour sessions at senior centers
  • Workshop Leading: Teach group classes on specific topics
  • Tech Support Hotline: Answer questions by phone
  • Device Setup: Help configure smartphones/tablets

Tech Buddy Matching

Our unique matching system pairs volunteers with seniors based on:

  • Shared interests (gardening, cooking, etc.)
  • Technology learning goals
  • Availability and location

Matches meet weekly for 6 weeks (in-person or Zoom).

Common Teaching Topics

  • Video calling (Zoom, FaceTime)
  • Online shopping & banking
  • Social media basics
  • Using healthcare portals
  • Smartphone photography
  • Spotify & podcast apps
