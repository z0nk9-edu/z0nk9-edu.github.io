City Bike Mechanic Crew

Keeping our community moving on two wheels

About This Program

Our City Bike Mechanic Crew provides free bicycle repairs and maintenance for low-income residents while promoting sustainable transportation. Since 2019, we've repaired over 1,800 bicycles and organized 65+ community cycling events.

Volunteer Activities

  • Bike Repairs: Perform basic maintenance and repairs on donated bikes
  • Workshops: Teach bike maintenance skills to community members
  • Bike Tours: Lead guided cycling tours of city landmarks
  • Donation Drives: Collect and refurbish used bicycles

Bike Sponsorship Program

Sponsor a bicycle for $150 and receive:
