Our Volunteer Programs

Urban Garden Stewardship

Environmental Learn More

City Bike Mechanic Crew

Repair and maintain bicycles for low-income residents. Organize bike tours and city exploration events.

Transportation Learn More

Digital Literacy for Seniors

Help seniors learn to use technology to connect with families and access digital services.

Education Learn More

City Cleanup & Art Mural Initiative

Why Volunteer With Us

Interactive Volunteer Map

Find opportunities near you with our color-coded interactive map showing different types of volunteer work.

Rewards Program

Earn "green points" and badges for your volunteer work that can be redeemed for local discounts.

Real-Time Registration

Sign up for events instantly and receive reminders to keep you organized and committed.

Volunteer Challenges

Community Forum

Multilingual Support
