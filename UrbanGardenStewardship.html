Urban Garden Stewardship

Growing community through urban agriculture

About This Program

Our Urban Garden Stewardship program transforms underutilized city spaces into vibrant community gardens and urban farms. Since 2018, we've converted over 45 vacant lots into productive green spaces that provide fresh produce to local residents while creating gathering spaces that strengthen neighborhood bonds.

Volunteer Activities

  • Garden Development: Help prepare new garden sites with raised beds, composting systems, and irrigation
  • Weekly Maintenance: Planting, weeding, harvesting, and general garden care
  • Education: Lead workshops on organic gardening, composting, and food preservation
  • Harvest Distribution: Package and distribute fresh produce to local food banks and shelters

Green Points Rewards

Earn 1 Green Point for every hour volunteered. Redeem points for:

  • 5 points: Seed packet or gardening gloves
  • 10 points: Herb growing kit
  • 20 points: 25% off at local nurseries
  • 50 points: Free gardening tool set
